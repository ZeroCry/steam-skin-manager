Window decorations

Window decorations are borders around all application windows. They contain close, minimize, maximize buttons and a title.

Steam can use native decorations or no decorations, which is the default behaviour. To use Steam with decorations, launch "Steam wb" from the dash or menu.

Warning!

Using Steam with native window decorations is an experimental feature and may not work or be buggy.
