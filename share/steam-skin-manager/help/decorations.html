Window decorations

Window decorations are borders around all application windows. They usually contain close, minimize, maximize buttons and a title.

Steam can't use native decorations on its own as it comes with it's own interface. Find out how to use Steam with system decorations in the launcher section.

Warning!

Using Steam with native window decorations is an experimental feature and may not work or be buggy.
